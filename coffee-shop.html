WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Looking for a cozy spot to take a break during your zoo adventure? Visit our Wild Things Coffee Shop, where you can unwind with a delicious coffee while enjoying the beauty of nature and wildlife all around you.

Why visit us:

  • Perfect for Animal Lovers: Watch the animals roam while sipping on your favorite brew! Our coffee shop is the ideal place to relax and take in the sights and sounds of the zoo.
  • Freshly Brewed Coffee & More: We offer a variety of high-quality coffees, teas, and delicious snacks. Whether you prefer a classic espresso, a creamy latte, or a refreshing iced drink, we’ve got you covered.
  • A Taste of Nature: Enjoy the taste of freshly brewed coffee in a serene setting surrounded by lush greenery and animal enclosures. It’s the perfect stop during your zoo visit!
  • Family-Friendly: The coffee shop is a welcoming space for families, offering a comfortable environment where parents can relax while kids enjoy the zoo.
  • Eco-Friendly Choices: We are committed to sustainability, offering eco-friendly cups and sourcing ethically grown coffee beans to ensure we’re doing our part for the planet.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

The Coffee Shop can be found to the east of the zoo, next to the koalas.
