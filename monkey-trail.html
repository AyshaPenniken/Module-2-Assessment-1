MONKEY TRAIL

Ready for an adventure? The Monkey Trail Obstacle Course is an exciting, hands-on experience where you can swing, climb, and jump just like a monkey! Navigate through a series of fun and challenging obstacles designed to test your agility and balance—perfect for thrill-seekers and families alike.

What to Expect:

  • Tree-Top Challenges: Climb ropes, swing across platforms, and balance on beams as you make your way through the course.
  • Fun for All Ages: The trail offers obstacles suitable for kids and adults, making it a great family activity.
  • Test Your Skills: Feel the rush as you navigate through jungle-themed obstacles—just like a monkey on a mission!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

The Monkey Trail is opposite the main entrance.
