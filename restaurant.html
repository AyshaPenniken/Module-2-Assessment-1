DINE @THE ZOO

Enjoy an unforgettable dining experience at Dine @The Zoo, where gourmet cuisine meets the beauty of the zoo’s natural surroundings. Located in the heart of the zoo, our restaurant offers a refined menu with locally sourced, seasonal ingredients, all served in a relaxing and unique setting.

Why Dine @The Zoo?

  • Exceptional Menu: Indulge in a variety of dishes, from fresh seafood and succulent steaks to vegetarian options and delicious desserts, all made with the finest ingredients.
  • Stunning Setting: Dine surrounded by lush greenery and wildlife, with both indoor and outdoor seating options offering views of the zoo's vibrant atmosphere.
  • Perfect for Any Occasion: Whether it’s a romantic dinner, a special celebration, or just a treat for yourself, Dine @The Zoo provides the perfect backdrop for any occasion.
  • Sustainably Sourced: We prioritise eco-friendly practices and use locally sourced produce and ethically sourced meats to create our dishes.
